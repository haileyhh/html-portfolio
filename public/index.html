The Best Movies According to Hailey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Goblin

This movie was filmed in South Korea and Canada (Quebec). It has amazing scenes of both countries and great storyline.

How I met your mother

The serie is based in New York and tells stories of a group of 5 friends, with the main story of how Ted Mosby met his wife.

Breaking Bad

This serie is about an ordinary chemistry teacher who discovered he got cancer and the journey of him becoming a drug lord.
